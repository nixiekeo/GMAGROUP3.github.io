Welcome to GMA!

Gen. Mariano Alvarez (GMA) is a dynamic and fast-growing municipality in the province of Cavite, located in the CALABARZON region of the Philippines. Known for its strategic location near Metro Manila, GMA serves as a vital link between the bustling urban centers of the National Capital Region and the expanding suburban areas of Cavite. The municipality is characterized by a blend of rural charm and urban development, making it an attractive place for both residential living and businesses.

GEOGRAPHICAL LOCATION

GMA has an elongated shape, about 7,040 meters long. Gen. Mariano Alvarez (GMA) is located in the province of Cavite, which is situated in the CALABARZON region of the Philippines. It lies in the southern part of Metro Manila, approximately 25 kilometers southwest of the National Capital Region. GMA is bordered by the municipalities of Dasmariñas to the north, Carmona to the east, and the city of Bacoor to the west. To the south, it is adjacent to the municipality of Silang. The municipality's strategic location places it within close proximity to key urban centers, making it a significant area for both residential and commercial developments. It is accessible via major roads like the Governor's Drive, which connects it to various parts of Cavite and Metro Manila.

BARANGAYS

Gen. Mariano Alvarez (GMA), a municipality located in the province of Cavite, is composed of 14 barangays. These barangays serve as the smallest administrative divisions within the municipality and play a crucial role in local governance, community services, and development initiatives. The barangays are integral to the municipality's social, cultural, and economic activities, with each having its unique characteristics and demographics. As part of Cavite's rapidly growing urban areas, GMA's barangays contribute significantly to the region's overall progress, providing residents with essential services, infrastructure, and opportunities for growth. As the community and town grew, 27 more barangays were established.
