Welcome to GMA!

Bayan ng Heneral Mariano Alvarez (GMA) is a municipality in Cavite, Philippines. Formerly part of Carmona, it began as the Carmona Resettlement Project managed by PHHC.

Geography

GMA has an elongated shape, about 7,040 meters long. It starts flat from the south and gradually becomes undulating in the northern barangays like Epifanio Malia and San Jose.

CALABARZON Provinces

  • Cavite
  • Laguna
  • Batangas
  • Rizal
  • Quezon

Creation of GMA

GMA became official after a plebiscite in 1980, as part of Batas Pambansa Blg. 75, separating it from Carmona.
